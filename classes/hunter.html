40 yard range
Instant

Fleshcraft
10 yard range
Channeled
2 minute cooldown
Form a shield of flesh and bone over 4 sec that absorbs damage equal to 20% of your maximum health for 2 min.

40 yard range
Instant
45 second cooldown

40 yard range
Instant
2 minute cooldown
